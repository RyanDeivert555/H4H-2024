Hydration Buddies

Reduce waste by finding refill stations nearby.

Home

Purpose

This website was created to help active people such as runners, bikers, and those walking to work to find quality drinking fountains and/or waterbottle refill stations. This allows users to rely less on single-use plastic bottles.

Authors

This website was created by ECLAIR - Elia, Crystal, Lori, Asha, Irene, and Ryan - students at Santa Clara University, for Hack for Humanity 2024.
