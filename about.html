ABOUT HYDRATION BUDDY

Reduce waste by finding refill stations nearby.

Purpose

This website was created to help active people such as runners, bikers, and those walking to work to find quality drinking fountains and/or waterbottle refill stations. This allows users to rely less on single-use plastic bottles. This website allows you to find high-quality drinking fountains, as rated by other users, as well as contribute ratings of your own. To submit a rating or view other ratings, sign in and then head to the Ratings tab above.
